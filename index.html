The Science Behind Getting It Done

Project Management

I've always been passionate about the relationship between good design and ideas (Art) and the mechanics behind getting things done right (Science).

The difference between the best idea in the world being simply adequate vs. being best-in-class comes down to how it's executed. When it comes to the planning, management, and execution of digital/creative products, I have over 20 years of experience of getting it done right.

I am a relentless process optimizer. If I see a way to make the thing we did today quicker and easier to do tomorrow — I do everything I can to make that happen. More often than not, a little bit of empathy for each and every stakeholder involved and a bespoke approach to what works for the team goes a long way.

Transforming Complexity Into Success

Often times, when balanced against budgets, timing constraints, and varying stakeholder expectations, making sure the process works for everybody takes a back seat. Knowing what the best solution is only half the battle — being able to implement it is the other (often more challenging) half.

I've seen first-hand what a little organization and process optimization can do for a project, team, product, company, and the bottom line. Click any case study to learn more.
